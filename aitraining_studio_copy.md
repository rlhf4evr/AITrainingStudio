## SECTION 7 — FAQ

**Section eyebrow:** Common Questions

**Headline:**
Everything you need to know.

**Q: Is this free for candidates?**
Yes, completely. We never charge candidates to access listings or use referral links. Our links benefit us when platforms track referrals — your cost is always zero.

**Q: What platforms do you list roles from?**
We curate opportunities from a vetted selection of leading AI training and evaluation platforms, updated as new high-paying networks emerge. Follow us on LinkedIn for platform-specific posts and referral links.

**Q: Do I need AI experience to apply?**
No. Most platforms are looking for domain expertise — being a strong doctor, coder, lawyer, or researcher matters more than knowing how AI works. Your professional background is the qualification.

**Q: How competitive are these roles?**
Selective. Platforms run their own assessments and make their own decisions. We don't influence outcomes or guarantee placement. What we do is make sure you find the right roles and apply with a referral link, which puts you in the fastest queue.

**Q: How often are listings updated?**
We review platforms regularly and add new listings as verified $100+/hr roles become available. Follow us on LinkedIn for real-time updates.

**Q: What if a role is no longer accepting applications?**
We remove or mark listings as closed as soon as we're notified. If you click a link that appears inactive, email us and we'll update it.
